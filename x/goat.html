A goat was purchased in your honor.

It’s part of Oxfam America’s exciting gift program that gives in two ways: by providing a symbolic gift for you as well as supporting people in need.

All kid-ding aside, goats are an invaluable resource for rural communities. A hardy goat is the gift of sustainability, which means fertilizer and food for families—especially those living in areas that cannot support less durable critters. The proceeds from this gift support Oxfam America’s efforts to right the wrongs of poverty, hunger, and injustice. As one of 17 members of the international Oxfam confederation, we work with people in more than 90 countries to create lasting solutions. Oxfam saves lives, develops long-term solutions to poverty, and campaigns for social change.
